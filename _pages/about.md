Hi! This is Xin Xia, a third-year Ph.D. student of Computer Science at The University of Queensland. I am jointly supervised by [A/Prof. Hongzhi Yin](https://sites.google.com/view/hongzhi-yin/home), and [Dr. Miao Xu](https://miaoxu-ml.github.io/index.html). I received my Bachelor degree at the School of Software (Elite Program), Jilin University. My research interests include recommender systems, sequence modeling, on-device learning, and self-supervised learning.
