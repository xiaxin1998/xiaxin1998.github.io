Xin XIA

Email: x dot xia at uq.edu.au

Hello, this is Xin Xia (夏新). I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at The University of Queensland, who works with Prof. Shane Culpepper. I received my PhD degree in 2023, specializing in Data Science at The University of Queensland, jointly supervised by A/Prof. Hongzhi Yin and Dr. Miao Xu. Prior to starting my PhD, I completed my Bachelor’s degree at Jilin University in 2019.

Research Interests

  • large language models
  • recommender systems
  • sequence modeling
  • self-supervised learning
  • on-device learning

Academic Services

  • PC Member: AAAI’23, CIKM’23, AAAI’24, WWW’25, etc.
  • Journal Reviewer: TKDE, TOIS, TNNLS, etc.
